InspireKick

Motivational Quote Generation Platform

HTMLCSSJavaScriptPHPMySQL
Problem

Motivation is ephemeral. People needed a reliable, distraction-free space to find curated inspiration on demand — without social media noise, paywalls, or algorithmic manipulation deciding their mood.

Solution

A clean, purpose-built motivational platform with a hand-curated database, dynamic randomization, and a UI designed to centre the content — not the interface. The experience is frictionless by design.

Key Features

Category-based filtering for targeted inspiration
One-click social share with pre-formatted output
Mobile-first responsive design with clean typography

02

QueueCare

Healthcare OPD Queue Management System

ReactNode.jsExpressMySQL
Problem

Hospital OPD queues are opaque, chaotic, and fail to prioritise critical patients. People wait hours without visibility — an inefficiency that degrades care quality, increases stress, and directly impacts patient outcomes.

Solution

A full-featured queue management portal with intelligent token generation, emergency triage prioritisation, and real-time queue tracking — bringing calm and transparency to one of healthcare's most stressful touchpoints.

Key Features
Smart token generation with emergency override prioritisation
Real-time queue position tracking and wait-time estimation
Appointment scheduling with intelligent conflict detection
Admin dashboard for live queue management and analytics
Impact
✦ Reduces average wait-time uncertainty by design
Live Demo GitHub
